## Business Context
Finance leaders require timely, accurate, and trusted visibility into budget performance, cost drivers, and overall spend composition to support decision-making, planning, and financial governance.

Operational data is often fragmented across cost centers, accounts, and time periods, making it difficult to:
- Assess whether the organization is operating within budget
- Identify cost centers and accounts driving variances
- Understand enterprise-level spend mix and utilization

This project simulates a SAP-style finance analytics environment and delivers structured dashboards to address these needs.

---

## Objectives
The primary objectives of this project are to:
1. Provide an executive-level summary of budget vs actual performance
2. Enable cost-center and GL-level variance analysis for corrective action
3. Deliver an enterprise-wide view of spend composition and budget utilization

---

## Stakeholders
- Finance Leadership (CFO, VP Finance)
- FP&A Analysts
- Accounting & Operations Finance Partners

---

## Scope
### In Scope
- Actual vs Budget reporting
- Variance ($ and %) analysis
- Cost center drilldowns
- GL account variance drivers
- Spend mix by account type and department
- Year-to-date budget utilization

### Out of Scope
- Forecasting or predictive modeling
- Transaction-level journal entry analysis
- Real-time or streaming data integration

---

## Assumptions
- Data reflects finalized monthly financials
- Budget values represent approved baseline budgets
- Fiscal calendar is standardized (12 fiscal periods)
- Data accuracy and validation are assumed post-ingestion

---

## Deliverables
- Tableau dashboards:
  1. Executive Budget Overview
  2. Cost Center Drilldown
  3. Financial Control & Spend Mix
- Metric definitions and documentation
- GitHub repository with dashboards, data model, and documentation
